## Hello, Dear Developers 👋

Welcome to **Lanying (Blue Jay)** — building your next-generation intelligent chat and AI applications.

[Lanying](https://www.lanyingim.com/) (developed by the **MaximTop** team) is a next-generation intelligent chat cloud service. We provide minimalist cross-platform SDKs and out-of-the-box enterprise AI platforms. Our services utilize cloud-native technology and multi-cloud architecture, with private cloud options available on a monthly subscription.

Currently, **one out of every seven new smartphones shipped globally uses Lanying IM technology.**

---

### 🚀 Core Products & Services

* **Lanying IM**: A minimalist cross-platform Chat SDK, helping enterprises quickly add professional communication features to any app.
* **Lanying AI**: An out-of-the-box enterprise AI Agent platform for building business AI Agents, Knowledge Bases, and RAG services.
* **GrowAI**: A one-stop AI SEO tool designed to help you capture organic online traffic effortlessly.
* **ClawChat**: Keep your **OpenClaw 🦞** bot online across all channels, including iOS & Android apps, WeChat Mini-programs, Web, and H5.

### 🤖 Intelligent AI Integration

Lanying features built-in integration with Large Language Models (LLMs). Enterprises can directly activate and connect to models such as OpenAI ChatGPT, Microsoft Azure AI, and Baidu ERNIE Bot through our console. Utilize our **BlueVector Knowledge Base** to upload documents and customize your exclusive enterprise AI assistant.
